Research Vision

Research

Trustworthy AI & ML Security

Robust learning, adversarial ML, generative-AI security, deepfake detection, LLM trustworthiness, and secure AI deployment.

Biometrics & Privacy

Face, fingerprint, ECG, PPG, anti-spoofing, liveness detection, cancelable biometrics, biometric template protection, and fairness.

Cyber-Physical & Hardware Security

Side-channel analysis, hardware authentication, PUFs, IoT security, GPU robustness, and secure computing for resource-constrained systems.

Cyber-Physical & Hardware Security

AI-powered medical diagnosis, digital biomarkers, ECG and physiological signal analysis, Foundation models and multimodal learning integrating vision.
