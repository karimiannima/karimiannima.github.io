Research Interests

Research

My research focuses on developing secure, trustworthy, and privacy-preserving artificial intelligence systems that bridge machine learning, biometrics, cybersecurity, healthcare, and computer architecture.

Trustworthy AI & AI Security

Robust and reliable AI systems, adversarial machine learning, generative AI security, LLM safety, deepfake detection, model robustness, and privacy-preserving learning.

Multimodal Learning

Foundation models and multimodal learning integrating vision, language, audio, physiological signals, and sensor data for intelligent and secure decision making.

AI in Healthcare

AI-powered medical diagnosis, digital biomarkers, ECG and physiological signal analysis, remote health monitoring, and personalized healthcare.

Biometrics & Privacy

Face, fingerprint, ECG, PPG, liveness detection, anti-spoofing, biometric template protection, cancelable biometrics, and fairness-aware recognition.

Cybersecurity & Privacy

Authentication, identity management, privacy-preserving machine learning, secure IoT systems, cryptographic key generation, and cyber-physical security.

Hardware Security

Side-channel attacks, PUFs, secure hardware design, GPU robustness, hardware-assisted AI security, and trusted computing architectures.
